The Saltwater Aquarium
The salt water aquariums are a signature feature that defines the theme of Atlantis for our guests. There are two custom-built aquariums within Atlantis. The first one is located in the lobby behind the cashier and is approximately 500 gallons. The second tank is built into the main bar situated on the main floor and is approximately 650 gallons. Both tanks are beautiful focal points for our guests to enjoy during their visit. Both tanks hold a wide variety of beautiful and colorful saltwater reef fish such as angels, triggerfish, eels, and tangs. Also, there are sophisticated filtration systems that maintain the integrity of each marine environment.
